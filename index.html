Hi, I’m Tanishka Bhatera

No one trained me to do art. No rules, no boundaries. I just created from what I felt. I started with portraits and paint, then one day the pen just never stopped. I draw when I’m happy, angry, even when I’m confused. I don’t know everything about design yet, but I know how it makes me feel: alive.

“Each line I draw is a piece of me. This is not just art. This is survival.”
